The Centresource creative department has been busy lately! We've been working on tons of fun client projects as well as some secret Centresource work that we can't wait to reveal coming in the next couple of months.

This past spring we designed and developed a fun project called 'iART Health'. Sponsored by Health Teacher, iART Health is an art contest that gives students the opportunity to creatively illustrate how to making a healthy choice leads to physical, emotional and/or social health.

Our challenge presented to our team was how to display these hundreds of posters in a creative and organized way without feeling too busy. As the designer I knew I wanted to incorporate illustrations as well as a grid system to display the posters. Our developers did an amazing job using Isotope, a jQuery plugin, to create a dynamic and responsive grid system.
