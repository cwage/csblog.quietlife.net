Google has an awesome program up called the [Summer of Code](<https://web.archive.org/web/20050603030610/http://code.google.com/summerofcode.html>):

> This Summer, don't let your programming skills lie fallow...Use them for the greater good of Open Source Software and computer science! Google will provide a $4500 award to each student who successfully completes an open source project by the end of the Summer. (payment details can be found in FAQ) 

I am glad to see that Jabber is one of the projects listed. Peter Saint-Andre has a great [list of ideas](<https://web.archive.org/web/20051018144611/http://www.saint-andre.com/blog/2005-06.html#2005-06-01T16:59>) for Jabber project development.
