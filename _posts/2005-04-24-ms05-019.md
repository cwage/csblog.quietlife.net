There have been some rumblings about problems created by installing Microsoft's latest patch for XP, [MS05-019](<https://web.archive.org/web/20050425084535/http://www.microsoft.com/technet/security/bulletin/MS05-019.mspx>). Well, turns out the problem has been identified. Fyodor, of nmap authoring fame, has some [choice words](<https://web.archive.org/web/20050428031854/http://seclists.org/lists/nmap-hackers/2005/Apr-Jun/0000.html>) on Microsoft's decision to disable raw sockets. The solution, of course, is to upgrade to a server license.
