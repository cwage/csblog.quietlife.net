![](https://csblog.quietlife.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/dip-and-squeeze.jpg)I recently ate lunch at Chick-fil-a. When I reached for the ketchup, I was happily surprised to see the greatest invention known to man (Well, that's probably a stretch but...). Heinz has created the new "Dip & Squeeze" packaging. It's awesome. You can choose to either dip your fries or squeeze the ketchup out! All in one package. Brilliant! I have waited a lifetime for this technology to exist. No more messy fingers. No more squirting out ketchup on a dirty napkin (#gross). And the best part, the new Dip & Squeeze packages have the equivalent of 3 standard ketchup packs.

From a design and usability standpoint, the new Dip & Squeeze out performs the standard packs by far. Possibly light years. It's perfect for the messy eater or the person that often likes to dip his or her fries while they are driving. It's awesome. I don't know why no one else thought of this sooner.

In efforts to promote the new product, Heinz is traveling all across America giving out french fries to any one that wants to try their new product. AND!!! Today (March 4th from 2pm-4pm) Chick-fil-a is giving away FREE FRENCH FRIES to anyone that mentions the "Dip & Squeeze". Sweet! Click [here](<http://www.chick-fil-a.com/Ext/FreeFryPromo.html>) for the details.
