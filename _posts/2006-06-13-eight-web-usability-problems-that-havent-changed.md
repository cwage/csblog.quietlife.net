Jakob Neilsen, the Godfather of web usability, has co-authored a new book entitled "[Prioritizing Web Usability](<http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0321350316/sr=8-1/qid=1150224005/ref=pd_bbs_1/102-9289187-4272133?%5Fencoding=UTF8>)". WebMonkey has been kind enough to present an excerpt from the new book about [Eight Problems that Haven't Changed](<http://www.webmonkey.com/webmonkey/06/24/index4a.html>). These are eight of the original issues that they found with web usability between 1994 and 1999 that are still a problem today -- even though technology and design has immensely improved. A high quality read when you have a chance.
