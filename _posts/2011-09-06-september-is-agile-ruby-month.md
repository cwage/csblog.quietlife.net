[![](/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/ruby-hoedown1-150x150.png)](</wp-content/uploads/2011/09/ruby-hoedown1.png>)As some of you may know, our developer team recently attended the [Ruby Hoedown](<http://rubyhoedown.com/>), the South's gathering of Rubyists and developers. Our team had a great time and after talking about what we know versus what we learned, it inspired us to share our expertise with you. So...

...we've decided that September is Agile Ruby Month at Centresource! We'll use this space to dive into Agile development best practices, Ruby tricks and tips, and even give you some live case studies of Ruby projects we're working on. (That means we might still be talking about those into October and onwards - we're working on some pretty big things!)

So sit back and enjoy - all month long we'll talk about our sprints, our scrums, our super-smart developers and our preferences for working with Ruby. It's about to get nerdy up in here!
