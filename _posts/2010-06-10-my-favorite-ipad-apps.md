After a few months with the iPad, I've settled upon my favorite apps (thus far). I could go into deep detail as to WHY I like each of these, but I decided to save you the preaching. However, you should always remember that the value of many apps is how well you learn them & incorporate their value into your work flow. The best example: Evernote. You either get it (and love it) or you don't - kinda simple. So, without further adieu, here's my list:

  1. **Evernote:** Its more than an iPad app, its a fantastic note management system. Almost like my second brain. Great for receipts, ideas, passwords, random info that's important but has no place, etc.
  2. **Penultimate:** Great hand-writing app for notes.
  3. **IM+:** Great app for instant messaging across AOL, Yahoo, MSN, GTalk, Facebook, Skype, Twitter with Push Notifications
  4. **ToDo:** Great daily planner that integrates with the site, [www.Toodledo.com](<http://www.toodledo.com/>)
  5. **1000 Experiences:** Neat travel-idea app from Lonely Planet
  6. **iWork Apps (Keynote/Pages/Numbers):** Good apps to run/edit Powerpoint, Word Docs, and Spreadsheets. Keynote also outputs to VGA
  7. **ArtStudio:** Nice if you like drawing
  8. **WebMD:** Beautiful app to help you become a hypochondriac :)
  9. **Pulse:** Beautiful RSS reader, but only as good as the RSS feeds you read. If you read mostly commercial feeds, then it isn't as great because commercial places really limit the amount of data they'll share in a feed
  10. **Wikipanion:** Nice Free app for searching Wikipedia
  11. **Amazon:** My fav place to shop and the iPad app makes the experience smooth as silk
  12. **Goodreader:** Nice app that connects to your mail server and lots of other services for mobile file access. It can read PDF (but so can iBook) and many other file formats. I combine 'Dropbox' and Goodreader as my main method for reading lots of files/docs on my iPad.



And yes, I play games too - but mostly 2 player with my wife :) The most fun 2 player games (for me) are:

  1. **WTD:** Awesome 'Whats the Difference' game
  2. **Harbor Master:** Absolutely crazy when you're playing with two people
  3. **Word Slam:** Fun, but super intense. I start sweating when I play.
  4. **FriendSheep:** At first I thought it was lame, but its actually hilarious when you actually play. Moreover, its physically exhausting :)
  5. **CheckersHD:** Basic but fun. I like the fact that it enforces the 'Force Jump' rule
  6. **Air Hockey:** The board is a little short, but definitely fun.



Enjoy!
