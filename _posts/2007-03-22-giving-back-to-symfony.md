Recently CentreSource adopted [symfony](<https://web.archive.org/web/20070321152651/http://www.symfony-project.com/>) as our #1 all-star player for php web software development. We have always said we wanted to give back to the open source community, and now symfony has given us the push needed to do it. CentreSource and Sensio have partnered together to develop [symfony-forge.com](<https://web.archive.org/web/20070416003958/http://www.symfony-forge.com/>), a site dedicated to symfony plug in development. More details coming soon. If you are interested in contributing please e-mail jwage at centresource.com.
