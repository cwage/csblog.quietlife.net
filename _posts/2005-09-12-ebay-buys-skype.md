It's a [done deal](<http://today.reuters.com/news/newsArticle.aspx?type=technologyNews&storyID=2005-09-12T081656Z_01_HO227963_RTRIDST_0_TECH-SKYPE-EBAY-DC.XML>):

> eBay has agreed to buy Skype. EBay plans initially to pay $1.3 billion in cash and $1.3 billion in stock and to make a further payout of up to $1.5 billion by 2008 or 2009 if financial targets are met, giving the deal a total value of up to $4.1 billion, a source said. Both eBay and Skype declined to comment. 

There goes the theory that google was ramping up its instant messaging/XMPP support as a precursor to buying Skype.
