We're proud (and excited) to announce that we are winners of two Achievement in Marketing Awards from the Nashville American Marketing Association.

Or, in shortspeak: we won two AIM's from NAMA.

The first was for **'Online Social Media Campaign, B2C'** where we detailed the campaign that led us into the finalists for the .Net Design Agency of the Year Award this year (you may remember this [social networking push](<http://blog.centresource.com/2010/11/10/centreforce-net-wwf-challenge/>) around the time of the nominations.)

The Second was for '**Marketing Campaign, B2C Medium Budget'** for our work with [NeighborMD](<http://neighbormd.com/>) (check out the case study and details [here.](<http://blog.centresource.com/2011/04/07/newly-launched-neighbor-md/>))

We're thrilled to have been considered and excited to be seated among [these other great winners](<http://nashvilleama.org/aimawards/>)!
