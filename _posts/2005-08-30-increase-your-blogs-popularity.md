Since the creation of our blog, CentreSource's page rank ([click here for explanation](<https://web.archive.org/web/20050830221820/http://www.iprcom.com/papers/pagerank/>)) has shot up from 0 to a 6 in a span of just 3 months. Since we know that our corporate website is not the source of all our popularity, we are positive the blog accounts for [this dramatic change](<http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/tg/detail/-/B000269C38/ref=alx_dp_tr_1/103-1255826-9859034?v=glance&vi=traffic&url=http://centresource.com/>).

Besides constantly sending article links to our friends/clients - we also spent a lot of time registering our blog with the most popular aggregators. It took me a long time to search and register with each one... But I lucked out and [found this list](<https://web.archive.org/web/20050830225432/http://www.faganfinder.com/blogs/>)!
