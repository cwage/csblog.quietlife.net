Greetings all,  
I've returned from Jupiter & brought with me Pixel Peach .com. I find everything about this site to be inspiring. The music is very appropriate to the content, & even features a music player. I think what heavily contributes to this beautifully ethereal site is the fact that the team consists of a flash designer & a photographer, & they're each phenomenal at their crafts. (There are some great photos on their old site, particularly under "stock".)  
Among all the clever navigation & little "easter egg-y" glitter, there's one of those click-drag-page-turn gadgets, which some people love! (nlh)
