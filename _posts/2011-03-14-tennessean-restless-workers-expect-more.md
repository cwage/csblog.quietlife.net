We're very excited to welcome Michael Calhoun to our development team. In fact, even _The Tennessean_ noticed that [we are a pretty cool place to work](<http://www.tennessean.com/article/20110304/BUSINESS01/103040332/Restless-workers-expect-more>).

> _Locally, computer programmer Mike Calhoun, 29, in the midst of switching jobs, said he was drawn to a new workplace where he can be more creative, earn higher pay and have a better opportunity to advance._
> 
> "There's much more room for innovation," he said of his new job at Centresource Interactive Agency in Nashville's Germantown neighborhood.
> 
> He doesn't have huge complaints about his current employer, a large institution in town, although his work has been largely limited to records management, and raises were minimal.
> 
> When the number of IT postings started to pick up, Calhoun said, he knew it would be a good time to make a move.
