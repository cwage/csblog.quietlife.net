[KickApps.com](<http://www.kickapps.com/>) has released a new whitepaper outlining [nine essential steps to creating a successful online community](<http://www.kickapps.com/get-started/resources.php>). For everyone who has ever wanted to create the next "Facebook Killer", here's your guide! All jokes aside, it's easy to come up with an idea for a social network -- after all, we're naturally social creatures. However, the problem lies not in the idea but in its execution. There's no magic recipe to make your idea successful, but follow these guidelines and you'll at least have a good foundation to build on. Good luck!
