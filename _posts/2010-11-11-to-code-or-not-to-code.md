One of our design wizards creative artists, Jason O'Brien was quoted in [an article in Six Revisions ](<https://web.archive.org/web/20101111/http://goo.gl/FjNip>) last week among industry giants Jeffrey Zeldman and Elliot Jay Stocks.

  


O'Brien, lovingly referred to as Shamus around here, weighed in on the article's posed question,

  


**"Should knowing HTML and CSS be required for web designers?"**

**  
**

Jason responded to the query via Twitter with,

  


> "If you're just pushing out PSDs, you're making a picture of a website. It's not actual web design until it's being rendered by a browser, interactivity and all."
> 
>   
> 

A lively debate continues in the article, with varying sources, polls and opinions on the hot topic.
