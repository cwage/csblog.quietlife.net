On Friday, we found out that our team won **NINE** Horizon Interactive Awards! [The Horizon Awards](<https://web.archive.org/web/20090602210156/http://www.horizoninteractiveawards.com/about.htm>) aim to recognize and commend the best work being done in the interactive community. They receive entries from all over the world, so to win even **one** of their awards is a huge honor. We entered some of our recent interactive work in to several of their categories, and came away with awards in online advertising, self-promotion, and entertainment among others! A big thanks has to go out to our awesome clients whose openness and collaboration really made these projects award-winning. Here's a list of our clients and projects that received awards:

**Gold Awards:**

[Southwestern Company  
Thomas Miller & Partners  
](<https://web.archive.org/web/20090603021642/http://www.tmpartners.com/>)[Yamaha: All Access 360](<https://web.archive.org/web/20090602070911/http://www.yamaha.com/allaccess360/>)

**Bronze Awards:**

[ALOC Group](<https://web.archive.org/web/20090310235855/http://www.alocgroup.com/>)  
[Belmont University: Debate 08](<https://web.archive.org/web/20090530065638/http://www.belmontdebate08.com/>)  
[Giving Tree: Give Card Incentives promotion video](<https://web.archive.org/web/20080919093226/http://www.givingtreeincentives.com/features.html>)  
[Golden Kraut](<https://web.archive.org/web/20090227164705/http://www.thegoldenkraut.com/>)  
[SHOUTAmerica](<https://web.archive.org/web/20090530061125/http://www.shoutamerica.org/>)  
[Trevecca University](<https://web.archive.org/web/20090307042801/http://www.trevecca.edu/>)  
As our team continues to grow in strength, I'm confident that this is just the beginning and there are more awards to come! Sure, we have to pull late nights sometimes.. but it's a great feeling being commended (nine times over!) for the work we are doing in the interactive world. We are working on a TON of really awesome projects right now that I can't wait to see entered for more Horizons next year. Here in the creative department, we make it a point to not let projects out of our door until we've asked ourselves: "Is this an award-winning design?" With goals like that, there will hopefully be many more blog posts in the future announcing achievements.

Good job, team. Give yourselves a pat on the back. :)
