Today is my one month anniversary here at Centresource. Before I was hired here, I was a full-time freelancer for two years (I still do some freelance and run a mobile web company with my wife called [Gallerytopia](<http://gallerytopia.com/>).) In my interview with [Chip Hayner](<http://www.centresource.com/who-we-are/meet-the-team#chiphayner>) and [Jason Jones](<http://www.centresource.com/who-we-are/meet-the-team#jasonjones>), they told me about the "R.O.W.E. system". What the heck is the R.O.W.E. System? It stands for "**R** esults **O** nly **W** ork **E** nvironment." ROWE - Where people are paid for productivity, not time spent "at work".  
Check out [GOrowe.com](<http://gorowe.com/>) to read about it. Here's the basics (and my favorite parts in order from top to bottom):

  1. Work from home if you want to (except for meetings of course)
  2. No set work hours (as long as you are available via phone, text message, email, or IM from 8a - 6p)
  3. Since you don't see your co-workers everyday, you can wear the same outfit two days in row if you want to.



After I was told about R.O.W.E. in my interview, I thought to myself, "Hmmm....this sounds a lot like what I've been doing for the past two years as a freelancer." It sounded too good to be true. Here's what I have observed about the R.O.W.E. system in my first 30 days here at Centresource:

The people here seem very relaxed and at the same time, very much at the top of their game. I've worked at other places and on my first day on those jobs, I could always spot the person that was completely burned out and wanted to go postal on everyone. I don't get that impression here. And maybe it's because of the R.O.W.E. system or maybe it's because the people that work at Centresource really like working here. It's been really easy getting to know everyone. They've all given me a warm welcome. With the R.O.W.E. it feels like we are all focused on our clients rather than getting to work "on time".

Going from full-time freelance to a job has been a very easy transition for me. I'm not going lie. I was a little nervous about jumping back into the job force again. Freelancing was such an awesome experience for me and I loved it. It allowed me to grow as a person and business man. With R.O.W.E., I still have the same feelings as I did when I was working for myself from home. In addition to R.O.W.E., I feel like Centresource has created a work environment like no other.

I highly recommend "Going R.O.W.E".
