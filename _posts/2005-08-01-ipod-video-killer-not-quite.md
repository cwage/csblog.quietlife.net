Business 2.0 [clued me into](<http://business2.blogs.com/business2blog/2005/08/the_antiipod.html>) a neat little device that could be (as they said) a nice little competitor to the iPod Video. The ZVue MP4 player has adequate [specifications ](<http://www.zvue.com/specifications.html>)& and the advantage of being first to the market. Moreover, they are in 900 Wal-Mart stores - so that will give them a great leg up on competitors.

Interestingly, its $149 price point is not THAT inexpensive when you consider that you must purchase an SD Card. A 1GB SD Card will cost you [approximately $80.00](<http://froogle.google.com/froogle?cat=436&q=SD+Storage+1GB&btnG=Search+Froogle&tiled=1>) from a reputable vendor - bringing the realistic price to around $230.

_08/03/05 Update: It appears the price (at the time of this post) was actually less than $100, thus making the cost around $180 if you add a 1GB SD Card_
