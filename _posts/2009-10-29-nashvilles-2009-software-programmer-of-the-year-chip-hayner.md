![NTC Programmer of the Year: Chip Hayner](/wp-content/uploads/2009/10/AwesomeGuitar-225x300.jpg)Our Director of Technology [Chip Hayner](</author/chayner/>) was named the [Software Programmer of the Year](<https://web.archive.org/web/20091116194632/http://www.technologycouncil.com/2009/10/28/nashville-technology-award-winners/>) by the Nashville Technology Council at an award reception this past Tuesday. The picture is of perhaps one of the coolest trophies we have ever seen: a custom designed Gibson/Epiphone electric guitar!

CentreSource Interactive Agency was honored with four total nominations, including Technology Organization of the Year, Innovator of the Year for our Founder/CEO [Nicholas Holland](</2009/10/author/nholland/>), who also earned a nomination for the Lifetime Achievement Award[](</author/nholland/>). Our hats are off to all of the nominees and winners for the work they do to make this an incredible city with a thriving technology community.
