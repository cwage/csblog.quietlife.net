Ah, the proliferation of digital devices! Doesn't it seem that everyone has shiny new TREO or BLACKBERRY that keeps them connected to their email 24/7? Could the world move any faster to a spammer's paradise?

I recently attended a seminar, "Get Motivated", which had all sorts of A-Type personalities seeking to glean knowledge from the likes of Zig Ziglar, Tom Hopkins, and Rudy Giuliani.

During the sessions, I was struck by the sheer volume of people 'typing' on their phones during the sessions. They weren't taking notes - they were still working!!! How's that for a super espresso shot of work/motivation...

With our intimate ties to the Anti-Spam industry through Swirbo, it becomes even more apparent how much spam will end up costing the world in lost productivity & wasted resource.

IDC estimates that by 2008, there will be 130 million 'Internet' enabled devices actively collecting email and spam! **Even more shocking, Ferris Research estimates that 500 MILLION SPAM MESSAGES will go to cell phones this year.**

Read it people... 500 million spam messages going to cell phones this year.
