Interesting, looks like Opera is adding [support for bittorrent](<http://operawatch.blogspot.com/2005/07/opera-adds-support-for-bit-torrent.html>). In the windows version, anyway. I wonder if they'll do the same for the linux version. I am not sure this is a good move. bittorrent is a complicated protocol that people will want a feature-rich client for, I think. Is it worth trying to cram this into a browser, making it even more bloated? I have not been too impressed with Opera 8.x so far. I hope it doesn't get worse from here on out.
