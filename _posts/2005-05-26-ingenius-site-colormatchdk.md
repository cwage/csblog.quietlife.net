Designers around the world know that color combinations are a MAJOR part of any design project. For artists, they have the ability to use color wheels and other physical devices to determine the best colors for their projects. In digital art, finding the right color combinations are just as paramount.

CentreSource, being a design firm, really loves the ColorMatch website to help choose complimentary colors. If you're ever in need of a 6 color combination for things such a GUI design, Logo Creation, and Webdesign... check out [http://www.colormatch.dk](<http://www.colormatch.dk/>)
