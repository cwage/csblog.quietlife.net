Our team recently had the pleasure of partnering [![LEAD](http://blog.centresource.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/LEAD-High-Black-Ring-copy.png)](<http://blog.centresource.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/LEAD-High-Black-Ring-copy.png> "LEAD")with one of Nashville's awesome charter school systems, [LEAD Public Schools](<http://www.leadpublicschools.org/>). Their vision is aggressive: to graduate 100% of their students and send them to a four-year college. Their board, staff, and teachers are dedicated, and their impact continues to expand throughout the greater Nashville area. With that, came a specific need in building a website for them - they needed not just one, but five sites, for their current school campuses.

Rather than doing custom sites for each of their current campuses, our dev team chose to work with the Aegir system, which enabled us to duplicate, or clone, one main site into several, using the same design template. This option worked out fabulously for our client, and they will now be able to clone sites at will as they continue to expand. We used the Drupal 7 CMS platform, empowering the client to edit numerous sites with ease and independence. LEAD chose to compliment the functionality of the sites with some beautiful images of students in their element on campus, field trips, etc., which in my opinion, was the icing on the cake. Seeing genuine smiles authentically portrayed is the perfect invitation to all five of their current sites.

Makes me miss junior high a little.

OK. Maybe not that much. But I'm very proud of our team to have launched five sites for LEAD Schools!
