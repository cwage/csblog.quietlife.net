The much anticipated [PHP Doctrine ORM](<https://web.archive.org/web/20071019230437/http://doctrine.pengus.net/>) is nearing a stable release. After a long [Google Summer of Code](<https://web.archive.org/web/20070710113623/http://code.google.com/soc/2007/>), Konsta Vesterinen, also known as zYne- in IRC, has tentatively scheduled the first release candidate for August 31st. This is very exciting for the [PHP](<https://web.archive.org/web/20070712012804/http://www.php.net/>) community as it fills a gap that has existed in [PHP](<https://web.archive.org/web/20070712012804/http://www.php.net/>) since its existence. Stay tuned for more information on this great news.
