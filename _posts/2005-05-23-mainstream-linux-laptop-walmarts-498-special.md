date: 2005-05-23
---
The hardware specs aren't magnificent, but I can't complain. The Linspire 498 is a complete laptop system that will provide all basic computing functions such as email, PIM (personal information management), browsing, Instant Messaging, OpenOffice, and pre-installed file viewers such as Adobe reader.

For icing on the cake, Linspire is throwing in a three month subscription to their CNR (click n' run) database - full of 2000+ download-able applications that automatically install and run on the Linspire laptop.
