Congratulations to us! [CentreSource ](<https://web.archive.org/web/20060321104409/http://www.centresource.com/>)was recently nominated for two categories (Emerging Business and Businesses with 1-25 employees) in the [Nashville Business Journal's](<http://nashville.bizjournals.com/>) Best in Business awards! I (Nicholas Holland) was also honored as a nominee for Entrepreneur of the Year! While being nominated is quite an honor, we are pleased to announce that CentreSource is now one of the [finalists for the NBJ: Best in Business - Emerging Business category!](<https://web.archive.org/web/20060626215949/http://nashville.bizjournals.com/nashville/stories/2006/03/06/daily29.html>)

We are truly honored and want to send a special congratulations to all the finalists - good luck!
