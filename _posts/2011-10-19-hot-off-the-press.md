If you haven't heard of OpenSky, you should [![](https://csblog.quietlife.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/open-sky.jpg)](<https://csblog.quietlife.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/open-sky.jpg>)definitely check them out. They're one of the most innovative eCommerce companies around and they got that way by allowing consumers to create their own unique shopping experience. As an expert and professional shopper myself, I can't think of a cooler idea! What's ironic about CentreSource as an Interactive Agency and OpenSky as an eCommerce site, is that our recent project with them was all about PRINT! Many of you may know Barcamp just passed and since it is always one of the leading interactive events of the year, OpenSky wanted to get their name out there and sometimes print is still the best way to do it. So for those of you who attended Barcamp on Saturday, you probably saw the OpenSky marketing cards. (Oh yeah, and they're hiring!). When looking for face to face communication, a simple print piece can be eternal. 

Be sure to check them out online at: <https://web.archive.org/web/20111019/http://opensky.com/barcampnashville2011>
