Nothing will make you more nervous than switching your entire business model to support a new technology :) Particularly when that technology is ONLY supported by the Open Source community. Regardless, it looks like we were right again - or at least not way off the beaten path.

Over a year ago, our team of super geniuses recommended we use [Symfony](<http://www.symfony-project.org/>)/[Doctrine ](<http://doctrine.pengus.net/>)as our MVC Framework & ORM. They also recommended we switch to [Drupal ](<https://web.archive.org/web/20071125103616/http://drupal.org/>)for our CMS platform. After 3-6 months, we learned that Yahoo had been quietly using Symfony for their internal PHP app framework!!! Now, we have more good news :) [Dries Buytaert](<https://web.archive.org/web/20071127034434/http://buytaert.net/>) had a good post letting us know that [Yahoo is using Drupal](<https://web.archive.org/web/20071130124407/http://buytaert.net/yahoo-using-drupal>) to create some of their retail sites. Check out [Yahoo Research](<http://research.yahoo.com/>) to see Drupal in action.
