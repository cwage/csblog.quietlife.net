We are very excited to announce that we recently won three 2015 [W3 Awards](<https://www.w3award.com/>)! The W3 Awards recognize creative excellence on the web, honoring outstanding websites, web marketing, web video, social content, and mobile sites and applications. These awards are sanctioned and judged by the [Academy of Interactive and Visual Arts](<http://aiva.org/>), a body consisting of top-tier professionals from acclaimed interactive, advertising, marketing and media firms.

Of the over 4,800 entries, only 10% were selected as gold winners, with other outstanding entries receiving silver accolades. We were awarded gold honors for our web work with The Seed Company, as well as silver honors for our work with both Christ College and Your Leadership Edge. Congratulations to our teams who worked on these projects and to all of the other outstanding project winners!
