title: "Moveover Gmail, others are offering 1TB of Storage"
date: 2005-06-12
---
Just like everything else on the Internet, it only takes 1 pioneer to draw others along. I [recently posted about GmailFS](</2005/06/12/gmail-filesystem-gmail-mounted-storage/>) (mounting your Gmail account as a storage drive) - but now an unlikely hosting service is offering 1 Terabyte (1 TB) of free online storage for email, files, etc. Hellacious Riders (yes, its an Auto & Bike Search Engine / Hosting Company) is [offering 1 TB limit email accounts](<https://web.archive.org/web/20050624082202/http://www.hriders.com/web_page.cfm?web_pageID=38>).

Through a post string ([see here](<https://web.archive.org/web/20050614013217/http://www.linuxquestions.org/questions/history/269897>)), I also learned that others have successfully mounted that drive using a Java program. While they say its slow - they've actually moved closed to 1 TB of data onto the server.

I'm sure more will follow soon!
