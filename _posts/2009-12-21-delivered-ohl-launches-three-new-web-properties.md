[![OHL](https://csblog.quietlife.net/wp-content/uploads/2009/12/OHL-300x199.png)](<https://web.archive.org/web/20100208194426/http://www.ohl.com/>)When a leader in global supply chain solutions contacted us about overhauling their Web site, our top priority was to make sure that we delivered on their expectations. That is why we are proud to announce the launch of the new [OHL.com](<https://web.archive.org/web/20100208194426/http://www.ohl.com/>), [OHL Excellence](<https://web.archive.org/web/20091224170417/http://www.excellence.ohl.com/>) and the [OHL Media Center](<https://web.archive.org/web/20091226050448/http://media.ohl.com/>).

[Ozburn-Hessey Logistics (OHL)](<https://web.archive.org/web/20100208194426/http://www.ohl.com/>) specializes in third party logistics (3PL) services, meaning that they partner with other businesses to meet their supply chain demands on a rapidly scalable platform. Our goal for the site was to communicate nearly 60 years of experience in a way that appeals to logistics decision makers. One of the ways that we achieved this is by producing a video that you can view right on their home page. The video features two people representing very different businesses, but one shared need -- a partner for their global distribution network.

The OHL Excellence and Media Center sites were created to cater to the specific needs of different segments of visitors to the site. [Excellence](<https://web.archive.org/web/20091224170417/http://www.excellence.ohl.com/>) highlights OHL's mission and values, corporate culture and awards with which they have been honored for being a global leader in their marketplace. The [Media Center](<https://web.archive.org/web/20091226050448/http://media.ohl.com/>) serves as a central location to find out about news and events for the company.

Visit the site at [http://www.ohl.com](<https://web.archive.org/web/20100208194426/http://www.ohl.com/>).
