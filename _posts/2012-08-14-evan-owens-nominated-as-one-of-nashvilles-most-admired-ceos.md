BIG NEWS!!! We couldn't[![](/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/MostAdmiredCEOlogochasecrosslin-175x300.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2012/08/MostAdmiredCEOlogochasecrosslin.jpg>) be more excited and proud of our very own Evan Owens being nominated as one of Nashville most admired CEO's!

The Nashville Business Journal, and its presenting sponsor, Chase, are pleased to announce the first annual Most Admired CEOs and Their Companies Awards program.

Per the description on the nomination:

"Finding a leader who inspires those around him or her is priceless. They hold their employees, products, and services in the highest regard, run their companies with integrity and are shining examples of how all companies should be run and managed. With that in mind we proudly present our first annual Most Admired CEOs and Their Companies Presented by Chase awards and dinner program."

The final votes will be submitted by mid-August so stay tuned!
