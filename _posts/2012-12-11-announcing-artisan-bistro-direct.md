[![Artisan Bistro](/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/artisanbistro.png)](</wp-content/uploads/2012/12/artisanbistro.png> "Artisan Bistro")Here at Centresource we try to pair our team members with projects that they would truly love and recently I got to share in that love. As interns come and go, they get wiser and wiser to my tricks. My current record is zero for every intern we've ever had when it comes to convincing them to get me lunch. I think my other teammates have realized this. When The Food Collective contacted us to build them an eCommerce solution for their new division, it was a match made in heaven. [Artisan Bistro Direct](<http://www.artisanbistrodirect.com/>) is their newest product line offering. They specialize in making inexpensive, chef-inspired bistro-style pre-made meals. The meals are made with all natural, organic, sustainable ingredients that are not only healthful, but actually taste good. How do you know this? Well, I "accidentally" received a case of their meals. I think everyone at Centresource would agree, they taste amazing! 

Our goal with this site was to market their products with the use of amazing photography showing just how delicious their meals look. We were also challenged with the task of making an eCommerce solution that was intuitive and easy for users of all skill levels. That challenge was made easier with the use of the Drupal 7 CMS platform. This site is not only beautiful, but its ease of use is sure to attract clients of all shapes and sizes. 

Everyone that was involved in this project deserves a big hand for all their hard work, including [Neesvig's Food Service](<http://www.neesvigs.com/>), which worked with us to seamlessly tap into their fullfilment service. As we sit down to enjoy a celebratory Artisan Bistro Direct meal, rest assured that no interns were invited to join us.
