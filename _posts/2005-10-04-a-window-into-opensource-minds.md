Marcus Whitney made some [interesting comments](<http://www.marcuswhitney.com/?p=24>) about OpenSource software and the mentality/motivation of those that criticize it. While I agree more with his notion of 'OpenSource is a Gift', his article inspired a flurry of comment activity with opinions on multiple sides.

When it comes to OpenSource, I think the safe approach is to 'Try at your own risk' - if you want SLA's and Assurances, you gotta pay the piper :)
