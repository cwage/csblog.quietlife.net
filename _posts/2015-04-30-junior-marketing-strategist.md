###### JOB TITLE

Junior Marketing Strategist

###### SUMMARY

The Junior Marketing Strategist is a marketing professional with a strong passion for digital. This role will involve capturing recommendations, findings and strategies from the team and from previous experience, and clearly communicating these concepts through written communication -- both emails and more formal reports. The ideal candidate for this role is somebody who is willing to jump into a project and get their hands dirty, and doesn't feel like any task is below them. The Junior Marketing Strategist will need to be a fast learner with a baseline knowledge of website analytics and social media, together with strong writing skills.

###### RESPONSIBILITIES

What activities will be expected in this role?

  * Author blogs and white papers on various topics
  * Conduct keyword research, and locate opportunities to increase inbound traffic to clients' websites
  * Learn to make backend changes to client websites related to search engine optimization, paid advertising and conversion optimization objectives
  * Monitor Google Analytics, Google Webmaster Tools and other third-party data sources to ensure engagement and success metrics are hit
  * Collaborate with internal teams to cross-promote search engine optimization, paid media advertising, content/social media marketing, website conversion optimization and other digital marketing initiatives
  * Understanding of client's business, marketing goals and digital ecosystem
  * This role requires a passion for, and willingness to learn, SEM, SEO, website optimization, social media, CRM and analytics
  * This role also requires a willingness to learn display advertising, testing methodologies, user experience and video for the web.
  * Assist with new business pitches
  * Audit, correct and launch third-party citation listings to fortify local SEO strategies



###### QUALIFICATIONS

What prior experience should you have?

  * 4-year degree, pursuing a degree or equivalent work experience in online or offline marketing
  * Strong writing and communication skills
  * Excellent organizational and project management abilities
  * Ability to multitask and handle multiple projects simultaneously
  * Passionate about digital marketing and a willingness to learn and work in a fast-paced, ever-changing environment



###### BONUS QUALIFICATIONS

(In other words, any knowledge or experience you have with these would be super-cool!)

  * Understanding of Photoshop
  * Coding chops or experience with Content Management Systems (WordPress, SquareSpace, etc)
  * Knowledge of basic HTML code
  * A working knowledge of web analytics tools
  * Google AdWords or Analytics certified



Are you a good fit? We hope so. To apply, send a message and your resume to [jobs@centresource.com](<mailto:jobs@centresource.com>) with I Love Marketing! in the subject line.
