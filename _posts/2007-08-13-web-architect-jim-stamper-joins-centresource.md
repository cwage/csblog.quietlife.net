title: "Web Architect Jim Stamper Joins CentreSource"
date: 2007-08-13
---
CentreSource Inc. is pleased to announce that **Jim Stamper** has joined the custom web design firm as Web Architect.

"We are very happy to have Jim join our growing team," said **Nicholas Holland** , CEO / President of CentreSource Inc. "His experience in print media and web design, combined with his reputation for customer service, is a welcomed resource to our firm. Our wide range of in house capabilities allows us to provide web design and back end solutions for our enterprise clients."

"It's exciting to be a part of such an incredibly talented team," said Jim. "CentreSource understands a client's need for web solutions and great design, and I am pleased to be a part of the firm's rapid growth."

Before joining CentreSource, Jim was freelancing at SouthSide Media Print and Web Design for the last 7 years, and served as Art Director for _Tour Guide Magazine_ , the largest live event production industry magazine.

His wealth of skill sets include expertise in PHP, XHTML, MySQL, CSS, Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Flash, and ActionScript.

In his down time, Jim can be found on a nearby lake on a sailboat or at home playing guitar.
