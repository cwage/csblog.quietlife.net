By now, you have read 13 million blogs and tweets about New Year's Resolutions.

What to do, what not to do, why they are important, why they are pointless.

So don't worry, we are NOT going to blog about our New Year's Resolutions.

Ok yes we are.

But only because we want to fully disclose some pretty personal and particular goals of ours as a firm this year. In hopes that as the year goes by and we check these off of the list, you can all look back and say, "Hey, they set out to do ____ and they totally nailed it!"

Or something like that.

So without further ado, here are our Resolutions/To-Do's/Wish List for 2011:

  * Score Two National Campaigns
  * Land One Campaign for a Feature Film Release
  * Earn a 'Best in Show' Award
  * Be Recognized for the Nashville Future 50 Award
  * Have an Article Published in a National Magazine
  * Publish One Industry Book



What are yours? We'd love to hear them.
