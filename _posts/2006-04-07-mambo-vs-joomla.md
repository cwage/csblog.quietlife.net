As we use and deploy more CMS software, we have been increasingly wondering what the differences exactly between Mambo and Joomla are. I knew that Joomla was a fork of Mambo, but I wasn't clear on why they forked. Fortunately, I found this [excellent article](<https://web.archive.org/web/20060303182530/http://www.devshed.com/c/a/BrainDump/Joomla-is-the-New-Mambo/>) that takes a tour of the turmoil and history surrounding Mambo and Joomla.

Next I hope to find (or write!) a more comprehensive comparison of the direction that the two teams intend to take with the software.
