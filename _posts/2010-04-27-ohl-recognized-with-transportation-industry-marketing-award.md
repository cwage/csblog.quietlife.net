![OHL Compass Award](http://blog.centresource.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/04/ohl-compas-award-237x300.jpg)CentreSource is proud to announce that another one of our clients has been recognized for excellence in their interactive marketing. The team at [Ozburn-Hessey Logistics](<http://www.ohl.com/>) (OHL) has been selected to receive an [Award of Merit from the Transportation Marketing & Communications Association](<http://www.tmcatoday.org/Awards/Awards.asp>). TMCA is the only association serving transportation marketing, sales, and communications pros in all modes and market segments of the North American transportation industry. The association describes the The TMCA Compass Award as "the highest level of professional acknowledgment within marketing and communication in today's transportation marketplace."

The entry was in the Web-Based Marketing category, as OHL had recently [worked with CentreSource to redesign several of their Internet properties](<http://blog.centresource.com/2009/12/21/delivered-ohl-launches-three-new-web-properties/>). You can read the [case study](<http://www.centresource.com/who-we-work-with#ohl>) on our site.
