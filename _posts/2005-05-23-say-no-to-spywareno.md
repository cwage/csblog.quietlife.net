Looks like the good folks at SpywareWarrioer have uncovered yet another piece of [spyware posing anti-spyware software](<https://web.archive.org/web/20060926150040/http://netrn.net/spywareblog/archives/2005/05/22/new-rogue-anti-spyware-spypareno/>), in this case one called SpywareNo.

So, if you see this software, be wary. It's not what it says it is.
