One of the best things about working at Centresource is our [ROWE](<http://blog.centresource.com/2011/03/30/rowe-part-1-journey-to-the-dark-side/>) policy. What is ROWE you may ask? ROWE stands for 'Results Only Work Environment'. Essentially ROWE is a management strategy where employees are evaluated on performance, not presence. That being said, many of our Centresource employees participate in this perk. Here are a few shots of where we've "ROWE'ed" the past week!

Designer, [Jeremy Mitchell](<http://www.centresource.com/who-we-are/meet-the-team#jeremymitchell>), ROWE'ed from Starbucks this morning checking his email with his baby girl on his lap while his wife Pinned things on pinterest via the iPad.
