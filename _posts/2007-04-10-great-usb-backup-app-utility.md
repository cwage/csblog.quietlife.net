I just found a great backup app (utility) for my USB drive. The app that comes with PortableApps isn't very flexible and it drove me to finding something better. Luckily, I found [Freebyte Backup](<https://web.archive.org/web/20070406191020/http://www.freebyte.com/fbbackup/>) through [PortableFreeware.com](<https://web.archive.org/web/20070407225740/http://www.portablefreeware.com/>). They even [told me how to 'make it more portable'](<https://web.archive.org/web/20070429174626/http://www.portablefreeware.com/?id=390>) by avoiding the installation and simply running the .EXE & creating my own profiles. Hope this helps others looking for a stable, feature-rich USB backup utility.
