The F-secure weblog [reports](<https://web.archive.org/web/20050524073412/http://www.f-secure.com/weblog/#00000563>) that though the Sober.Q worm appears to be checking web locations for new updates, none of the locations it is checking are available, so it may not get another chance for another round of infections.
