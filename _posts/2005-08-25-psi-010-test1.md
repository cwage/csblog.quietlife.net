[Psi](<https://web.archive.org/web/20050825205436/http://psi.affinix.com/>), a pretty decent Jabber client, has just released Psi-0.10-test1, and with this release, we finally get tabbed chat windows!

With this addition, it looks to be shaping up to be the best Jabber client I've seen by far -- I might make the switch from [Tkabber](<https://web.archive.org/web/20050826021728/http://tkabber.jabber.ru/>), which has long been my favorite Jabber client, but suffers from an inability to login to multiple accounts. (The website says it supports it, but I can't figure out how -- anyone know?)
