**2\. What are your inspirations?  
**I think most of my inspiration comes from the web. I spend a lot of time on Dribbble keeping up with current trends and getting an idea for what's next. A great source for what I have been inspired by would be my likes on Dribbble check them out [here](<http://dribbble.com/JoshSullivan/shots/likes>).

**3\. When designing, you feel____?  
**I feel challenged. Every time I start a design it gives me the ability to try something new. I get to chance to experiment with new design trends and expand my skills.

**4\. What's your favorite color?  
**About 4 different shades of grey. I would give you the hex values but they are a trade secret. Seriously I think grey is pretty great.

**5\. How do you continue to build your design 'tool belt'?  
**Most of my design resources come from other creatives. I get a lot from my newsreader and from other designers on twitter.

**6\. Do you have any advice to future designers?  
**Work with others. Learn from those with more experience. Don't tell your parents I said this, but if you go to school to become a web designer you might not get everything it takes to be successful. I feel like if could have done it over again I would have took all the money I spent on school and I would have went to all the big design conferences and interned at a different agency every 6 months.
