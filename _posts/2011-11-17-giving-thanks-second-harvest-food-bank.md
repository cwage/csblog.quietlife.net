Thanks to all those who came out to our November mixer! In honor of our last mixer of the year, we sponsored a food drive for [Second Harvest Food Bank of Middle Tennessee](<http://secondharvestmidtn.org/>) and ended up donating 81 pounds of food! Thanks to all that participated.

**Did you know that 1 in 6 Tennesseans struggle with hunger?**

[![](/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Donate_time_infograph-300x105.png)](</wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Donate_time_infograph.png> "Donate_time_infograph")

Thanks to you, your donations directly benefit Second Harvest Food Bank's Emergency Food Box program, which serves a crucial role in feeding hungry people during times of crisis. Just down the road from Centresource, Second Harvest Food Bank of Middle Tennessee is one of the largest and most comprehensive of over 200 food banks and food distribution centers nationwide. During the 2009/2010 fiscal year, Second Harvest distributed more than 53.3 million pounds of food to hungry men, women and children. We're pretty proud to call them our neighbors!

Here's to a fun year of mixers and we look forward to seeing you at a mixer in the new year!
