[![](https://csblog.quietlife.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/spotify-wow-300x233.png)](<https://csblog.quietlife.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/spotify-wow.png>)Last year, we had the opportunity to work with WOW, a collaboration of the top Christian and Gospel music from EMI Christian Music Group, Provident Label Group, and Word Entertainment, by redesigning [their website](<http://wowilovechristianmusic.com/>). This year, they asked us to come back and help them create an app for [Spotify](<http://spotify.com/>), an online music service.

The goal was to create a music discovery application that provided users a unique experience in finding the best in Christian and Gospel music. We're pretty excited with the results!

[Check out the new app -- now playing on Spotify](<http://smarturl.it/WOWapp?IQid=csblog>)!
