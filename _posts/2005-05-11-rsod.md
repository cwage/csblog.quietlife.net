It's long overdue, but it looks like with the introduction of Longhorn, Microsoft may be finally putting the famed [BSOD](<https://web.archive.org/web/20050513205033/http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blue_screen_of_death>) to bed for good.

That's right, folks, meet the [RSOD](<https://web.archive.org/web/20051102033123/http://blogs.msdn.com/michkap/archive/2005/05/07/415335.aspx>) -- the Red Screen of Death. Looks good, but let's hope it doesn't make as many appearances as the BSOD.
