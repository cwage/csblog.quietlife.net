[Google ](<http://www.google.com/>)definitely knew what they were doing when they launched [GMail](<http://www.gmail.com/>). An [article in NY1 News](<http://www.ny1.com/ny1/content/index.jsp?stid=101&aid=51217>) cites a recent AOL study that shows just how addicting email has become.

> The study shows Americans check their email nearly five times a day, and we spend on average about an hour a day just writing email, says Regina Lewis of AOL. Email users today rely on email as much as they do the phone, and three in four have more that one email account. We also can't be away from it very long. Thirty-six percent of respondents say they can't go more than two or three days without checking their email, and more than half, 60 percent, report they check their email even while on vacation.
