Eweek takes a fascinating look at how [the Red Cross is using VoIP](<http://www.eweek.com/article2/0,1895,1857523,00.asp>) telephony from [VSATs](<https://web.archive.org/web/20050816081651/http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Very_small_aperture_terminal>) in the field in lieu of a functional infrastructure to communicate. Pretty awesome technology and a good indicator how technology really can make a difference.
