I have been evangelizing a lot to our developers about the merits of [vim](<http://www.vim.org/>) lately.

Two good references I've found:

  * [VIM Cheat Sheet](<http://tnerual.eriogerg.free.fr/vim.html>) \- in various formats, including a compact 2-page PDF.
  * [VIM Tutorial](<http://aleph0.clarku.edu/text_2003/resources/vim_tutorial.pdf>) (PDF) - an **excellent** walkthrough of VIM from the basics to advanced usage. It's hefty at 500+ pages, but even going through the very beginning is very helpful for beginners. Even as a long-time user I have found things I never knew about in this document.
