Debian Administration had a [good HOWTO](<http://debian-administration.org/articles/238>) yesterday that covers the process of converting a system with 1 drive (with data) to a system with 2 drives in a RAID-1 config while keeping the data intact. Someone in the comments links to a good article about [the merits of swap on RAID-1](<http://www.linuxjournal.com/article/5898>), something about which I have often wondered as well.

One of these days I may try this myself..
