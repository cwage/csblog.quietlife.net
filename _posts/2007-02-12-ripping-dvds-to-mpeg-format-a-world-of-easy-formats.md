Shout out to my friend, Sunny Patel, for passing along [a great utility for both Windows and Mac users](<http://mediafork.dynalias.com/blog/?page_id=8>). Now everyone can rip their DVDs to an easier to use format, MPEG, thanks to a great program called [MediaFork](<http://mediafork.dynalias.com/blog/?page_id=8>) (formerly Hand Brake). It will rip DVDs to MPEG, thus making them MUCH easier to put on your iPod, Apple TV, generic Media Center, etc, etc. We are one step closer to making it easy to take your digital assets anywhere, anytime.
