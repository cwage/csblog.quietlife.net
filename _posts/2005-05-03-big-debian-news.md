We at CentreSource are huge proponents of the [Debian](<https://web.archive.org/web/20050501063655/http://www.debian.org/>) distribution of GNU/Linux, which is why it delights me to see that [Sarge (3.1)](<https://web.archive.org/web/20050507044559/http://www.debian.org/releases/testing/>) has [officially been frozen](<https://web.archive.org/web/20050507111705/http://lists.debian.org/debian-devel-announce/2005/05/msg00001.html>). No word on hell, yet, but I would expect it should follow closely after the pigs fly.

In all seriousness, Debian's release timelines are not one of its most popular facets, so it's good to see Sarge finally getting closer to getting out the door. They are targeting a May 30 release.
