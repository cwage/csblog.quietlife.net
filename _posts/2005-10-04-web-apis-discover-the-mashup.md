Thanks to [Rex Hammock's post](<http://www.rexblog.com/2005/10/03#a8357>) about another Google Maps [tweak](<http://maps.huge.info/>), I discovered the link to a '[Mashup](<http://www.programmableweb.com/mashups>)' website that combines various web API's with unintended uses. My particular favorite was '[Adactio Elsewhere](<http://elsewhere.adactio.com/>)' - its really neat to see someone utilize AJAX to incorporate sites like [Upcoming](<http://upcoming.org/services/api/>), [Amazon](<http://www.amazon.com/gp/browse.html/ref=smm_sn_aws/002-4614567-4431257?%5Fencoding=UTF8&node=3435361>), [Flickr](<http://www.flickr.com/services/>), and [Del.icio.us](<http://del.icio.us/doc/api>).
