So, Microsoft last week announced they were [buying spyware-giant Claria](<https://web.archive.org/web/20060926163558/http://netrn.net/spywareblog/archives/2005/06/30/microsoft-to-buy-adware-company/>). I had hoped maybe this was some bizarre move by Microsoft to buy them and shut them down -- you know, a gesture to the community or something.

Sadly, no, they appear to be doing something [much stupider](<https://web.archive.org/web/20060926173556/http://netrn.net/spywareblog/archives/2005/07/01/microsoft-antispyware-ignores-claria/>), as Microsoft's anti-spyware application now detects but "ignores" Claria's spyware. Very odd move by the beast in Redmond.
