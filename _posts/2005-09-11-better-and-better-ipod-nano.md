Just the other day I told a friend of mine that the iPod Mini was almost perfect. I told him that I really love the small size, its clear sound, and how much music it could hold. I, however, had two requests to make it even better... #1) I wanted it smaller... #2) I wanted it to handle my photos & video. Well, Apple's latest release, the [iPod Nano](<http://www.apple.com/ipodnano/>), takes care of #1 :) Now I just need to sit back and relax until they release their video versions!

*Note: My friend worried that the screen would be too small... If Cell Phones are moving in that direction (games, video, etc) - I think the Apple team can probably get it right.
