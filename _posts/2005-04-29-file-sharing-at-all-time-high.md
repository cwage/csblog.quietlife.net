The RIAA and MPAA have been very vocal about the effect their legal actions have had on the decreasing popularity of file-sharing. However, Slyck.com did a study that tells a [very different story](<http://slyck.com/news.php?story=763>), with P2P traffic nearly doubling since 2003.
