title: "Web Architect Matthew Stubblefield Joins CentreSource"
date: 2007-08-08
---
CentreSource Inc. is pleased to announce that **Matthew Stubblefield** has joined the custom web design firm as Web Architect, effective August 8, 2007. 

"We are very happy to have Matthew join our talented team," said **Nicholas Holland** , CEO / President of CentreSource Inc. "His experience in developing web-based applications, combined with his network and systems engineering background, is a welcomed asset to our firm. His experience enhances our ability to serve our clients' objectives to embrace and incorporate web solutions quickly."

"I am very honored about joining this great firm," said Matthew. "CentreSource has developed a national reputation for web solutions and design, and I am pleased to be a part of the firm's rapid growth." 

Before joining CentreSource, Matthew served for three years as owner and systems engineer of Middle Tennessee IT, providing IT consulting, services, and support. 

His wealth of skill sets include fluency in ASP, HTML, PHP, XML, CSS, PERL/CGI, Visual Basic Legacy, VB.Net, VBScript, JavaScript and WSH. Software packages include Microsoft Visual Studio Legacy, Visual Studio .NET 2003, Visual Studio .NET 2005, IIS Server and Exchange Server. Matthew traces back his knowledge of Windows servers and technologies when he hacked his grandmother's 8086 PC at age 7.

In his spare time, Matthew enjoys writing entire musical compositions using electronic technology, spinning the hits as a performance DJ, and spending time with his son Brayden.
