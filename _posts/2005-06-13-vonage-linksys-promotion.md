Linksys is running a promotion along with Vonage which looks to be a [pretty good deal](<http://www.eweek.com/article2/0,1759,1826704,00.asp>), if you've been wanting to try out Vonage's service anyway:

> Vonage and router-manufacturer Linksys have partnered to create what they hope will be an offer customers can't refuse. Starting on June 12, and for six weeks after, consumers will be able to trade in any router for one of Linksys' Wireless-G routers with two phone ports for VOIP for free after-rebates, when signing up for Vonage's phone service. 

The router is a new one of Linksys's, which comes with two ports for phones along with the normal DSL/router 802.11b/g functionality. It comes pre-configured for the Vonage service.
