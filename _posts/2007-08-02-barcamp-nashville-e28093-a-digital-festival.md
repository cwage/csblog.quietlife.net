The Exit/In has been a music fixture in Nashville dating back to the seventies. In the shadows of Vanderbilt University behind the facade of black painted walls on Elliston Place - known as "The Rock Block" in the old days - lies The Exit/In where many talented acts have launched their careers while performing over the years. From comedian Steve Martin to Jimmy Buffett, John Prine and Linda Ronstadt, musicians of all genres have made late night memories over the storied thirty-six year history of the club.

On August 18, The Exit/In plays host to **BarCamp Nashville**. [What is a BarCamp?](<https://web.archive.org/web/20071017175132/http://www.barcampnashville.com/blog/about/>) It is a tech unconference - meaning that when you register to attend (for free), there is an expectation to bring your experiences and involvement in the interactive, music, design and marketing communities to share information. In other words, YOU are key to participating with peers to raising the level of excellence and shaping the future in Nashville's growing digital community.

CentreSource is proud to be a Silver Sponsor of BarCamp Nashville. Three of our team members will be sharing as speakers - Chris Wage on _Demystifying Web 2.0_ , Jared Scheel on _Exploring Apple's iPhone Development using iTosser_ and Jonathan Wage discussing _Enterprise PHP - Symfony and Doctrine ORM Frameworks_.

If you're too young to experience being a Jimmy Buffett Parrot Head from the early days at The Exit/In, then be a part of BarCamp Nashville! Whether you describe yourself as a Tech Head or Gear Head doesn't matter; there's no better place to take hold of the new future of new media, online marketing, digital technology, music and more. Why? Derive satisfaction knowing that by attending, you're making an investment in Nashville's digital community as a _participant_ rather than a _spectator_. 

Rock the Block - digitally - at The Exit/In and add to the history.
