An interesting partnership has developed between [KDE and Wikipedia](<http://meta.wikimedia.org/wiki/KDE_and_Wikipedia>), in order to develop somewhat of an API for applications to snag content directly from Wikipedia:

> We want to provide our desktop users with a way to easily access Wikipedia content. At the same time we want to create a technology that developers of other projects can draw from. Presenting: the KDE Wikipedia integration. This is good for KDE, but also for everyone else, since the en:Webservice en:API will be open for everyone.
