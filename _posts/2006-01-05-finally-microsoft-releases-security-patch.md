Chris [told everyone](<https://web.archive.org/web/20060104065007/http://blog.centresource.com/2006/01/02/more-wmf/>) that the flaw in the MS Windows XP operating system was serious. In fact, he was amazed at the seriousness of the flaw and how little Microsoft was doing to fix it. According to [this AP article](<http://news.yahoo.com/s/ap/20060105/ap_on_hi_te/microsoft_security;_ylt=Am5UORH2_9vuv1_uEIgB8ahU.3QA;_ylu=X3oDMTA4ZnRnZjhkBHNlYwMxNjk1>), Microsoft has finally released a patch (much later than other security entities). If our clients had to patch to our software ([http://www.swirbo.com](<https://web.archive.org/web/20051223090521/http://www.swirbo.com/>), [http://www.dekkotime.com](<https://web.archive.org/web/20060106061651/http://www.dekkotime.com/>)) before we did, I can't imagine us staying in business very long. Oh the joys of a monopoly!
