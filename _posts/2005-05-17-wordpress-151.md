For those of you that use WordPress, note that there's a [cross-site scripting vulnerability](<https://web.archive.org/web/20120124072736/http://www.securityfocus.com/bid/13664>) and a [SQL injection](<https://web.archive.org/web/20111004141646/http://www.securityfocus.com/bid/13655>) vulnerability in 1.5 that has been resolved in the newest minor release, 1.5.1, which you can get [here](<https://web.archive.org/web/20050518005314/http://wordpress.org/download/>).
