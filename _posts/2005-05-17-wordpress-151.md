For those of you that use WordPress, note that there's a [cross-site scripting vulnerability](<http://www.securityfocus.com/bid/13664>) and a [SQL injection](<http://www.securityfocus.com/bid/13655>) vulnerability in 1.5 that has been resolved in the newest minor release, 1.5.1, which you can get [here](<http://wordpress.org/download/>).
