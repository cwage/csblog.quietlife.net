Today I would like to bring a little bit of art into your world by looking at our country through graphic design. Fifty designers from fifty different states have constructed a project called '[Fifty and Fifty](<http://statemottosproject.com/gallery/>)'. The concept of this project is that each designer will illustrate their historic state motto by adding their own creative and modern spin to the design.

The project began in Nov of 2010 and is almost complete. Here are few examples of my favorite designs thus far...the Texas design *almost* makes me want to go to Texas. I'm still holding out for Tennessee and will purchase one immediately!
