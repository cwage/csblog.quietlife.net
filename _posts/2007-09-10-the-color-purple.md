**Find the Perfect Colors for Your Website**

> Color choice is a crucial decision in every website design. Sometimes you may be designing a site for an existing business that already has an established color scheme, but many design projects will require development of a color scheme. Fortunately, there are almost endless resources online to help you find that perfect color combination. 

Go take a look at [www.vandelaydesign.com](<https://web.archive.org/web/20070921175007/http://www.vandelaydesign.com/blog/design/find-the-perfect-colors-for-your-website/>)

By the way, the color Purple combines the stability of blue and the energy of red. Purple is associated with royalty. It symbolizes power, nobility, luxury, and ambition. It conveys wealth and extravagance. Purple is associated with wisdom, dignity, independence, creativity, mystery, and magic. [Source](<http://www.color-wheel-pro.com/color-meaning.html>)
