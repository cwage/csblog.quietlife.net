Here ye, here ye: the [Nashville Technology Council](<http://www.technologycouncil.com/>) officially released the 2011  _Techville_ map!

What's more, this year centresource got to partner with them in creating the [interactive version.](<http://techville.us/>)

Our very own Nicholas Holland describes _Techville_ as a "unique tool in its ability to give an overview of the diverse & vibrant technology community in Nashville. It is helpful to job seekers, B2B sales teams, and even for choosing areas that have the strongest vendors in the region."

_Techville_ is a self-published technology map featuring a visual depiction of the technology organizations that make up the Nashville tech ecosystem.

This year's map includes the geographical location of local companies and the industry connections featuring a variety of industries: healthcare, education, data center, telecommunications, consulting, accounting, SEO, creative, government, legal, staffing tech products and services, and security.

Centresource helped create the [interactive edition](<http://techville.us/>), which extends the brand through a GIS based search application using Microsoft Silverlight and Bing Maps.

Check out the site and to order printed copies of Techville, visit http://www.technologycouncil.com.
