Another cool addition is [sasyncd](<http://www.openbsd.org/cgi-bin/man.cgi?query=sasyncd&sektion=8&apropos=0&manpath=OpenBSD+Current&arch=i386>), which, although not quite ready for use yet, will allow you to sync IPSEC security associations between load-balanced redundant OpenBSD servers along with [carp](<http://www.openbsd.org/cgi-bin/man.cgi?query=carp&sektion=4&apropos=0&manpath=OpenBSD+Current&arch=i386>) and [pfsync](<http://www.openbsd.org/cgi-bin/man.cgi?query=pfsync&apropos=0&sektion=0&manpath=OpenBSD+Current&arch=i386&format=html>).
