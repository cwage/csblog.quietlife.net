In this town, when I say 'singer' you think of a dozen of your closest friends and their hopes and dreams.

But around the Centresource office, when we say Singer, we think of a client whose website we are very proud of.

And it seems we aren't the only ones who like the look of the new site. 

Craftzine.com-an online magazine reaching 1 million readers each month-liked the new Singer header so much they chose to featured Singer on their homepage to promote the worlds largest DIY festival, Maker Faire.

Forgive us if we unabashedly highlight this achievement for Singer but at the end of the day it's more about them than us-we achieve success when we get to stand behind great companies and help them do what they do best, better.

We're like a great pair of pantyhose-who's beauty lies not in getting noticed, but rather in how well they highlight a great pair of legs.

So here's to Singer Sewing Co.: a great pair of legs.

Nick is going to kill me for that comparison.
