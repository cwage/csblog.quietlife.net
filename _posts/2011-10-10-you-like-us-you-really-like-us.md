Everyone here at Centresource was pleasantly surprised to discover the Nashville Scene has picked our mixers as the Best Casual Geek Hang in Nashville!

![](http://www.nashvillescene.com/imager/best-of-nashville-2011-heroes-and-villians/b/original/2644724/5ed5/coverstory1-1.jpg)

The [writeup](<http://www.nashvillescene.com/nashville/best-of-nashville-2011-people-and-places-writers-choice/Content?oid=2644714>) sums up our intention for our bi-monthly Interactive Mixers perfectly:

  
What sets the "interactive web and mobile marketing" firm apart...is a casual vibe that doesn't feel cliquey or like a networking meat market.  


Couldn't have said it better ourselves. We strive for a fun and casual environment for friends to gather and form new connections. No pressure, no awkward ice breakers... just fun.

Speaking of our mixers, don't miss our next get-together on November 3rd. [Be sure to RSVP](<http://centresource.pingg.com/November2011>), and bring some canned food for our friends at [Second Harvest Food Bank](<http://secondharvestmidtn.org/>) who will be in attendance. We'll have more great food and of course, great free beer from friends like [Yazoo](<http://yazoobrew.com/>) and more. And hey -- they both made the Best of 2011 list, too! (Second Harvest won Best Non-Profit and Yazoo won Best Brewhouse - not too shabby!)

We're thrilled to share this list with so many great people and places around our favorite city. Again, big thanks are owed to [Lance Conzett](<http://twitter.com/#!/lanceco>) for the kind words and the humbling addition to this year's best and brightest roundup of Music City!
