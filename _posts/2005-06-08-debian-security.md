Don't go burning a copy of Debian 3.1 yet. Looks like they [forgot to uncomment](<http://www.advogato.org/person/vorlon/diary.html?start=11>) the entry for security.debian.org in /etc/apt/sources.list on the ISOs recently released.

This is of course a relatively minor issue -- all you have to do is uncomment the entry in /etc/apt/sources.list. But it's still an unfortunate oversight that some users may not catch and never enable. As Steve Langasek said: "Whoops".
