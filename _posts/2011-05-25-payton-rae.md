We have some exciting news about one of our recent projects!

Over the past few months we've been working on 15-year-old music sensation [Payton Rae's website](<http://www.paytonraemusic.com/>) as well as the strategy leading up to the release of her [first EP](<http://itunes.apple.com/us/artist/payton-rae/id435151077>).

Well, (drumroll please) the record went on sale last week and hit #4 on iTunes US and #8 iTunes Australia! She stayed at number 4 on iTunes in the US for 4 days and at #8 in Austrailia for 1 day.

This is a huge win for any new artist's first release.

Congratulations to Payton Rae, we can't wait to see what the future holds! You can buy her album [here](<http://itunes.apple.com/us/artist/payton-rae/id435151077>)!
