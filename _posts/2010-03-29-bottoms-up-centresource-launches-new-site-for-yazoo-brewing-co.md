This past Friday, [Yazoo Brewing Company](<https://web.archive.org/web/20100401220403/http://www.yazoobrew.com/>) held their grand opening for their new Taproom in Downtown Nashville. To coincide with the opening, we also launched their new Web site. The site gives the visitor an overview of the brewery and invites them to discover the many lines of beer that Yazoo has to offer. The visitor can also buy merchandise as well as find out the latest news from their blog and Twitter accounts.

Check out their site at **[www.yazoobrew.com](<https://web.archive.org/web/20100401220403/http://www.yazoobrew.com/>)**.
