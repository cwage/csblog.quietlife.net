![Silver Telly Award](http://blog.centresource.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/tellysilverlarge-199x300.jpg)

Our downstairs fireplace mantel is getting a bit more crowded. We picked up a [Silver Telly Award](<http://www.tellyawards.com/winners/>) statuette for our work in creating the online video for the [Two Futures Project](<http://www.twofuturesproject.org/>). This is the second major award for the site, the first a ["Best in Show" from the W3 Awards](<http://blog.centresource.com/2009/10/09/two-futures-project-centresource-honored-with-w3-best-in-show-award/>). The second Silver Telly Award is given for our work in creating a [video called "No Charity"](<http://blog.centresource.com/2009/12/01/centresource-and-bloodwater-mission-team-up-to-launch-video-on-world-aids-day/>) for [Blood:Water Mission](<http://www.bloodwatermission.com/>).

More than 11,000 entries from all 50 states and five continents were received for the 2010 competition, honoring the very best local, regional, and cable television commercials and programs, as well as the finest video and film productions and work created for the Web.
