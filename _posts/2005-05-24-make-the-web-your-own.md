With the millions of websites out there, you are bound to run into many sites that just don't work the way you would like them to. For those of us with the initiative, we can now take matters into our own hands. 

[GreaseMonkey](<https://web.archive.org/web/20050525004047/http://greasemonkey.mozdev.org/>), a [firefox](<https://web.archive.org/web/20051008155217/http://www.getfirefox.com/>) plug-in, allows users to create a 'user script'. You can use site specific scripts or scripts for all sites. If you want to have the feeling of control, but don't want to spend the time writing your own user-scripts there are a load of scripts ready for [download](<http://dunck.us/collab/GreaseMonkeyUserScripts#head-74c0fda1054b04bf3e2365d467e32a47e3feba7b>).

Some examples of what you can do with GreaseMonkey: 
  1. [Change currency displayed on ecommerce sites to display in your native currency](<http://www.ping.uio.no/~chlunde/stuff/amazon-currency.png>)
  2. [Show the age of an actor on IMDB](<https://web.archive.org/web/20050520140328/http://www.stewarts.org.uk/stuff/IMDBAge.png>)
  3. Remove annoying adds on geocities type sites
  4. Hide blog posts by authors that do not interest you
  5. Censor or replace selected words or phrases



The possibilities are endless!
