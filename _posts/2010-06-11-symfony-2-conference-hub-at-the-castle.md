When the Symfony Live Online conference was first announced, [Brandon](<http://www.centresource.com/who-we-are/meet-the-team#bvalentine>) came up with the great idea to get the entire Nashville Symfony/PHP community to watch it together at centresource. "It'll be an amazing gathering of nerds the likes of which have never been seen!" he says.

Well, Sensio decided to steal our thunder by suggesting [Symfony 2 Conference Hubs](<http://www.symfony-project.org/blog/2010/06/09/state-of-symfony-2-conference-hubs>) a few days later. Hey Sensio, we still take credit for the idea. We thought of it first. And now that's off my chest, the sales pitch:

The [Symfony Live 2010 Online Conference](<http://symfony-live.com/>) will explore the state of [Symfony 2](<http://github.com/symfony/symfony>). We encourage you to register online (for the low price of 25 dollars) and join us on June 23rd at 10:00 AM in the centresource conference room. We will watch everything on the projector, laugh, cry, and have a very emotionally rewarding experience together. The conference will run 3 hours, from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM.

A complete list of Symfony Live hubs can be found on the [symfony blog](<http://www.symfony-project.org/blog/2010/06/09/state-of-symfony-2-conference-hubs>). We hope to see you there!
