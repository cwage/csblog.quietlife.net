I'll admit it, I'm a devoted fan to the beloved funny youtube video but do you ever have information overload when you have to sift through the millions of videos to find that _one_ special video? Also, do you ever get frustrated with the quality of videos from time to time? Everywhere we look, we're bombarded with hit-or-miss video content through a fragmented design experience. Today I stumbled upon [**Vidque**](<http://vidque.com/>) which now makes watching videos online enjoyable and at a high quality.

Vidque's main goal is to establish a single platform that celebrates quality over quantity, with an emphasis on discovery and durability. Its a free curation platform designed to help discover, filter and archive online video content. Controlled and curated by its users, Vidque aims to simplify the discovery of quality video content through the joint effort of the online community.

[![](http://blog.centresource.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/Vidque.png)](<http://blog.centresource.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/Vidque.png> "Vidque")When you sign up with Vidque, each unique video that you post has its own video page that features the ability to have a discussion and a list of users that saved this video. This page could be a very clean, simple and high quality way to showcase your product or company without the noise of other video platforms. Welcome to the video platform club Vidque!
