[![Barcamp Nashville](http://blog.centresource.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/barcamp-300x71.gif)](<http://blog.centresource.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/barcamp.gif> "Barcamp Nashville")On October 1, [BarCamp Nashville](<http://www.barcampnashville.org/bcn12/>) announced its sessions for the October 20 event which celebrates all that is tech and marketing in Nashville, Tennessee. The unconference to end all unconferences is a yearly huzzah for the Centresource crew, and this year we are extremely excited about our representation at the event.

While the BarCamp [session selection is at random](<http://www.barcampnashville.org/bcn12/speak>), the Centresource team is representing strongly, and we are stoked! Here is a list of the Centresourcerers and their respective topics.

Evan Owens & Chip Hayner - [Stop Managing and Start Leading](<http://www.barcampnashville.org/bcn12/sessions/panel-stop-managing-and-start-leading>)  
Winston Hearn - [Coffeescript: Six Reasons and a Movie](<http://www.barcampnashville.org/bcn12/sessions/coffeescript-six-reasons-and-movie>)  
Micah Redding - [Saving History from the Data Monsters](<http://www.barcampnashville.org/bcn12/sessions/saving-history-data-monsters>)  
Mark Wise - [Building Music Applications with Javascript](<http://www.barcampnashville.org/bcn12/sessions/building-music-applications-javascript>)  
Jon Arnold & Centresource Panel - [Minecraft: The Game for Everyman (and Woman)](<http://www.barcampnashville.org/bcn12/sessions/panel-minecraft-game-everyman-and-woman>)  
Jeremy Holland - [A Crash Course in Crypto](<http://www.barcampnashville.org/bcn12/sessions/crash-course-crypto>)  
Nicholas Holland - [Sneak Peek & Early Access to http://populr.me](<http://www.barcampnashville.org/bcn12/sessions/product-development-401-lessons-learned-building-httppopulrme>)

We also had a hand in developing the [newly launched BarCamp Nashville website](<http://www.barcampnashville.org/bcn12/>). Shout out to our ace Drupal dev [Evan Willhite](<https://twitter.com/evanmwillhite>) for the awesome upgrades to the site.

If you're not yet signed up to attend the event, go ahead and [visit the sign up page](<http://www.barcampnashville.org/bcn12/user/register>), and we'll see you there on October 20!
