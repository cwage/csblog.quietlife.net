Scoble has [announced that Outlook 12 will include RSS reading capabilities](<https://web.archive.org/web/20051228050613/http://scobleizer.wordpress.com/2005/12/22/outlook-pm-talks-about-upcoming-rss-integration/>). This will result in an explosion of the amount of people who are reading RSS. Especially since RSS will also be integrated into IE, Firefox, and every other web browser.

This is a great time for companies to start getting their blogs ready as a way to be in continuous communication with customers. We have helped several companies launch blogs recently and I expect there will be many more in the coming months.
