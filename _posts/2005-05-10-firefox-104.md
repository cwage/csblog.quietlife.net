Details on these vulnerabilities can be found on [our post](<https://web.archive.org/web/20060112091749/http://blog.centresource.com/2005/05/09/firefox-vulnerability/>) as well as [this mozillazine](<https://web.archive.org/web/20050514040136/http://www.mozillazine.org/talkback.html?article=6590>) article.
