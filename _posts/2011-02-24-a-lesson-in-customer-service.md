Yesterday morning I was preparing for a client meeting and I had my iPad and my [iPad Bookarc by Twelvesouth](<http://twelvesouth.com/products/bookarc_ipad/>) with me. I was telling [Nick Holland](<http://www.centresource.com/who-we-are/meet-the-team#nicholasholland>) how I acquired my Bookarc. The story goes something like this:

It was late afternoon on a Saturday this past summer. My sister and I were shooting a wedding the very next day. I had the idea of using my iPad to showcase some images in a slideshow at the reception from the day's ceremony. The only problem was, I didn't have an iPad stand. There are lots of iPad stands to choose from but I only wanted the [Bookarc by Twelve South](<http://twelvesouth.com/products/bookarc_ipad/>). I went to the Apple Store and they were sold out. I drove over to Best Buy...no dice. I even called a few friends that I thought for sure would have one....they didn't. So as a last resort I tweeted this: "Looking for a Bookarc by Twelve South for a wedding that is happening tomorrow. #HELP"

The first and only response I received was via email from [Andrew Green, Founder and CEO of Twelve South](<http://twitter.com/andrewbg>). He said,

"Hey Jeremy,

Saw your tweet looking for our stuff. I actually have family in Nashville. One of them has a new BookArc for iPad : ) How bad do you need it? I'm sure I could arrange for you to pick it up. Can I help?

Andrew Green  
TwelveSouth  
Founder & Creative Director"

![](https://csblog.quietlife.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/bookarc-by-twelvesouth-247x300.jpg)

I quickly responded and he and I worked out the logistics over a few more emails. He arranged for me to pick up a brand new Bookarc from his mom...free of charge! Wait. Did I say free? Yep. Free. I said, "Let me at least pay you for this." He responded with, "Please accept this as my gift to you for going out of your way to find our BookArc. There are other choices of iPad stands out there - and we're glad you chose us. Have a great  
gig this evening and wish the Bride  
and Groom best wishes from Twelve South : )"

Wow! Now that is customer service. He did not have to do that. He could have ignored my Tweet and gone on with the rest his Saturday afternoon. Why would he give me one of his products for free? Why would he go out of his way to make sure I had a Bookarc for the wedding I was about to shoot? I believe this is why: Every time I get the chance to tell this story... I do. He probably knew that I would Tweet, Facebook, blog, and tell all of my friends about it. It made me more excited about his company. And I am now a customer for life.

So what?

How can you apply this story to your business?  
How can you create a success story based on an act of kindness?  
How can you be a rock star CEO like Andrew Green of Twelve South?  
He for sure challenged me to step up my customer service game.
