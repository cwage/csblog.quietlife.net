Dearest Twitter,

This is an official thank you note.

Not for the copious amount of mindless information available to me at all hours of the day.

Not for the introduction of hashtags into my life and, consequently, daily conversation.

No, not even for the endless platform with which to talk about social media ad nauseam.

What I want to thank you for is the acquired skill to write more concise, sharp, powerful, punchy content.

Because of your 140 character limit, I now know what excessive words I use mindlessly and recklessly. I now know how to focus on the punchline. I now get to my 'hook' without excess baggage. I can now construct google ads, mini bios, slogans, UI copy, taglines, jingles and catch phrases with ease and delight, knowing that even the fewest of words can have effective outcomes.

I now know better how to engage and impact the less-is-more, ADD, over-saturated consumer in a variety of new, bite-sized, micro, concise, focused ways.

So, thank you.

Now please can you do something about the pesky foursquare updates?

Sincerely,

Lyndsay

Micro Copywriter, Bite-Sized Brander, Selective Sloganist
