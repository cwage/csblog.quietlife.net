I'm a big fan of analogies and metaphors to help clarify a concept or bring understanding to technical subjects such as search engine optimization.

Dave Zuis uses "Physical Fitness" as a metaphor in his article at **SearchEngineJournal** \- [SEO & Physical Fitness Metaphors](<http://www.searchenginejournal.com/seo-physical-fitness-metaphors/5563/>)

I found Everett's comment to be absolutely spot on:

> _I am a firm believer that companies who are SERIOUS (I'm talking over 100 k per year serious) about their search engine optimization efforts need to either get an in-house SEO, an embedded consultant or both. Having some guy on the other side of the country with dozens of other clients send you a report that says "Do this, this and this..." without actually DOING any of the "doing" is like joining one of those online personal trainer websites where you pay someone to send you an exercise routine via email._
