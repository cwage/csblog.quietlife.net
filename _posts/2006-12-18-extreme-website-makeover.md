Those of you who read [our newsletter](<https://web.archive.org/web/20070723153459/http://www.centresource.com/thesource_200612>) know that for this Holiday season, CentreSource had a contest to find the website in the greatest need for a makeover. Companies were able to nominate their websites, which were then filtered by the CentreSource staff down to the 10 Top Worst Websites. Finally, the finalists were asked to launch their own grassroots campaigns and get their family, friends, and coworkers to vote for their site to win the grand prize.

In the end, [forAWE.com](<https://web.archive.org/web/20070106203059/http://www.forawe.com/>) beat out the [Houston County Area Chamber of Commerce](<https://web.archive.org/web/20061218031433/http://www.houstoncochamber.com/>) by a margin of 519 to 490 votes. 

Congratulations to forAWE.com, who will be receiving the following from CentreSource:

  * Design Consultation and Planning
  * Design Guide
  * 2 Home Page Design with revisions
  * 1 Interior Page Design with revisions
  * 1 Flash Element
  * Up to 10 pages of Content Placement & Styling
  * Quality Assurance
  * Site Deployment



If you're interested in future contests, please join [our mailing list](<http://e2ma.net/app/view:Join/signupId:6286/mailingId:350896>). Congratulations again to forAWE.com!
