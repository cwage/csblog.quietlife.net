Opera Watch [verifies](<https://web.archive.org/web/20060310130337/http://operawatch.blogspot.com/2005/10/bittorrent-to-be-included-in-opera-9.html>) that Opera 9.0 will include a bittorrent client.

I have mixed feelings. I hope that it won't add to Opera's increasing bloat and memory profile, but on the other hand, I think bittorrent is a great technology that [more people should use](</2005/06/20/bittorrent-and-security/>).
