One thing we enjoy doing here at the office is celebrating the successes of our clients, particularly when we are able to help meet their interactive goals. Ozburn-Hessey Logistics, LLC (OHL) was recognized today by the Nashville Chapter of the American Marketing Association (NAMA) for their website redesign project as a finalist for the Achievement in Marketing Awards in the Business-to-Business category.

On their blog, [Michelle McManus writes](<http://www.media.ohl.com/blog/2010/09/achievement-marketing-logistics-marketing-moment-worth-sharing>):

> Today, Karen Hall and I (Michelle McManus) attended the [Nashville American Marketing Association](<http://nashvilleama.org/> "Nashville American Marketing Association") Achievement in Marketing abbreviated award ceremony as [OHL](<http://www.ohl.com/> "Logistics") was selected as a finalist in the B2B category for our website redesign project. We launched late 2009 and have included many of the modern interactive capabilities and have a cleaner look as well as a more streamlined navigation. I encourage you to provide [feedback](<http://www.ohl.com/feedback> "Submit Feedback to OHL") about our site, [OHL.com](<http://www.ohl.com/> "Logistics"), as well as [Excellence.ohl.com](<http://www.excellence.ohl.com/> "Excellence in Logistics | OHL"), [Media.ohl.com](<http://www.media.ohl.com/> "Logistics News and Views at Global Logistics Company OHL").
> 
> I did not get a chance to deliver an acceptance speech but will gladly share what I intended to say here on this blog post as well as show you the beautiful recognition piece.
> 
> My speech, should I had delivered it, would have said:
> 
> It is with great honor that I accept this award on behalf of OHL. To be recognized by this organization is truly an achievement in marketing. I want to acknowledge the members of my team; you had the courage to think differently, making this honor possible. I also want to recognize our partner, [CentreSource](<http://www.centresource.com/> "Interactive Agency | CentreSource"). Chip Hayner and Aaron Briggs are two of the best people to ever have on your team. Aaron Briggs is one of the most detail oriented, strategic thinkers ever and Chip Hayner pulls rabbits out of hats for a living.
> 
> Thank you all so much.
> 
> ~Michelle McManus

We cannot possibly create world-class interactive solutions without the support of clients who are passionate about their business. We join OHL in celebrating their success, and cannot wait to see what the future holds!
