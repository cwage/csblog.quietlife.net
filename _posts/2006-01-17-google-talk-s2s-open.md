Google Talk s2s functionality is now [open for business](<https://web.archive.org/web/20060202015711/http://googletalk.blogspot.com/2006/01/xmpp-federation.html>). This is great news. s2s is functionality that enables a jabber server to talk to other jabber servers -- something that Google did not enable initially, much [to my chagrin](<https://web.archive.org/web/20060116022841/http://blog.centresource.com/2005/08/25/metaphor-time/>).

This is a great step in the right direction for the adoption of XMPP.

(Thanks to [Tenzil](<http://www.livejournal.com/users/tenzil/>) for the heads-up.)
