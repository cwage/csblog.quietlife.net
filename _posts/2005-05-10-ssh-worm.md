Bruce Schneier discusses the viability of a [SSH worm](<https://web.archive.org/web/20050511010715/http://www.schneier.com/blog/archives/2005/05/the_potential_f.html>) using the simple fact that ~/.ssh/known_hosts contains visited hostnames and keys, along with the fact that a compromised user/password on one machine will likely work on any of the other machines listed in ~/.ssh/known_hosts.
