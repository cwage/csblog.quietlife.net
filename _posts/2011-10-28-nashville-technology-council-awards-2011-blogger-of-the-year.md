__Last night the Nashville Technology Council held their[2011 "Feel the Beat" Awards Gala](<http://www.technologycouncil.com/2011/10/28/2011-awards-gala-winners/>), and boy are we blown away! Our dear [Lyndsay Rush](<http://twitter.com/rushbomb>) picked up the award for Social Media/Blogger of the Year. As if that wasn't enough, our own [Brandon Valentine](<http://twitter.com/bvalentine615>) was honored by being nominated for Software Engineer/Programmer of the Year.

Nashville has been a great home for Centresource over the years and we're thrilled that our team continues to be recognized by such a great tech community. Speaking of our awesome Nashville community, our November mixer is right around the corner (Thursday, November 3rd, to be exact) and we'd love to see you! [Be sure you RSVP](<http://centresource.pingg.com/November2011>) and bring a canned food item (or [digital donation](<http://vad.aidmatrix.org/vadxml.cfm?driveid=5490>)) to our friends at [Second Harvest Food Bank](<http://secondharvestmidtn.org/>). We hope to see you then, so come on by and enjoy a drink and some holiday cheer on us.
