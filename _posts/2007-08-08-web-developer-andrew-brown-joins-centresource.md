title: "Web Developer Andrew Brown Joins CentreSource"
date: 2007-08-08
---
CentreSource Inc. is pleased to announce that **Andrew Brown** , of Ezekiel Design has joined the custom web design firm as Web Developer / Graphic Designer, effective August 8, 2007. 

"We are glad to have Andrew join our dynamic team," said **Nicholas Holland** , CEO / President of CentreSource Inc. "His design portfolio, combined with his extensive background and content management systems experience, is a welcomed addition to our firm."

"I am very excited about joining this great firm," said Andrew. "CentreSource has developed a national reputation for its excellence in custom web design and solutions, and I am honored to be a part of the firm's continued growth." 

Before working with CentreSource, Andrew served for two years as owner of Ezekiel Design. His clients have included Warner Bros. / Word Entertainment, Elexio, various ministries, and recording artists. 

He brings a variety of skills to CentreSource, including HTML, XHTML, XML, JavaScript, PHP, Actionscript, CSS, Adobe PhotoShop, Adobe ImageReady, Macromedia Flash, Dreamweaver, MS Front Page, and CMS.

In his spare time, Andrew enjoys watching movies, swimming, playing with his 5-month-old daughter Jaedyn and spending every moment with wife Hannah.
