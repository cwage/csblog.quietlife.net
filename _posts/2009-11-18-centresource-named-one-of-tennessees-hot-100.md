[![¡En fuego!](http://blog.centresource.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/11/2009-Hot-100-Logo-223x300.png)](<https://web.archive.org/web/20091108124417/http://businesstn.com/content/200911/2009-ibusinesstni-hot100>)The weather may be quite a bit cooler than it was a month or so ago, but it is warm in our North Fourth office. Why? Because CentreSource [was named as one of the "Hot 100"](<https://web.archive.org/web/20091108124417/http://businesstn.com/content/200911/2009-ibusinesstni-hot100>) by _BusinessTN_ magazine. You can check out the story in the November/December issue (pg 24 to be exact) -- which happens to be the very center of the magazine. Coincidence? I think not! Get it...centresource - centerfold. (hehe)

The "Hot 100" list is a statewide Who's Who of the fastest growing, emerging and most promising companies in 2009. The editors use a variety of criteria for to make their selections, such as revenue, employee growth year-over-year, etc. As they put it, "the companies listed represent the best Tennessee has to offer in terms of entrepreneurial vision and growth."

We couldn't be more excited and honored by our selection!
