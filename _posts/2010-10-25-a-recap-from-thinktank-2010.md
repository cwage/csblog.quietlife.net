![ThinkTank 2010](http://blog.centresource.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/10/thinktank2010.png)

For me conferences are 1/2 wildly inspiring and 1/2 a judo chop to the throat on how much I have to learn. This past weekend's ThinkTank 2010 was no exception. Centresource's creative team is proud to be closely involved with our local chapter of the AIGA who puts on ThinkTank annually. This year's event brought together over 300 local design professionals and students to learn from some of our nation's leading creatives. This years speakers were:

  * Sean Adams - partner at [AdamsMorioka](<http://www.adamsmorioka.com/>) in Beverly Hills, and an amazing designer / part-time undercover superhero.
  * [Jessica Hische](<http://jessicahische.com/>) \- typographer and illustrator working in Brooklyn, New York, and more talented than I will ever be in my life! HOLY CRAP!
  * [Jina Bolton](<http://www.sushiandrobots.com/>) \- Creator of online journal Sushi & Robots and interaction designer at Engine Yard (http://www.engineyard.com/), not to mention Jason O'Brien's profesh-crush.
  * [Tasha French](<http://www.nashvillecontributor.org/>) \- Director and designer of The Contributor, Nashville's only street newspaper.



It was an amazing day of inspiration and networking. If you are looking for a great organization to be a part of, we highly recommend the [Nashville AIGA](<http://nashville.aiga.org/>). There are really great things going on in our local creative community! Huge props & thanks to all of the centresource creative team who volunteered who in turn helped make this year's ThinkTank a HUGE success.
