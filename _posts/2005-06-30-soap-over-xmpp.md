JEP-00072 [SOAP Over SMPP](<https://web.archive.org/web/20050622083620/http://www.jabber.org/jeps/jep-0072.html>) has been released. Looks pretty cool. SOAP is, as the JEP says, "agnostic" about its transport protocol, although it's typically used over HTTP. Using it over XMPP might have some interesting possibilities.
