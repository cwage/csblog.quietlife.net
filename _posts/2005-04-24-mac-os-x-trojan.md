On Friday, I [wrote](<http://blog.centresource.com/2005/04/22/security-by-obscurity/>):

> Some users even tout their security advantages with a rather grandiose utopian delusion of permanency. It won't last forever. Mac OS X and GNU/Linux continue to gain market share on the desktop, but the true mark of their success, unfortunately, will likely come in the form of the first widespread malicious software. 

I didn't think it would be so soon! Sophos has recently listed a profile of a [trojan for Mac OS X](<http://www.sophos.com/virusinfo/analyses/maccowhanda.html>). Okay, so it's not so widespread. Details are lacking, and I don't know if it's even been spotted in the wild at all or not.
