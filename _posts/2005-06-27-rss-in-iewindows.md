This is old news by now, but I waited until all the rumors were sorted out. Looks like Windows/IE will officially [support RSS](<http://www.freep.com/news/latestnews/pm4757_20050624.htm>). Responses have varied -- some [less than ecstatic](<https://web.archive.org/web/20051124073722/http://www.johnmora.net/2005/06/25/longhorn-and-rss-imitation-not-innovation/>) and some [more ambivalent](<http://www.jaxn.org/blog/archives/910-Microsoft-is-Playing-Catch-Up-with-Open-Source.html>). Personally I think it's a good step, but not very exciting, really. It is however a great opportunity to make snide jokes at Microsoft's expense, like for example that this will provide a standard, streamlined way to feed exploit information to compromised Windows botnets all over the world!
