With Christmas quickly approaching, we felt it would be helpful to list some great website that assist with holiday deal finding! Highlighting the marriage of technology and great deals/coupons, we've listed a few sites for you to start your hunting!

[http://www.fatwallet.com](<http://www.fatwallet.com/>) \- This site not only lists deals & coupons, it has also worked out 'Cash Back' deals with hundreds of online merchants. Members of FatWallet get varying percentages of Cash Back when they shop at a Fat Wallet store - and Fat Wallet periodically sends checks back to them. Some percentages are as high as 25%!  
**Comments:** This site is fantastic! Not only does it provide you with coupon codes, it also provides you with a cash back incentive! If you're going to shop in the first place, this is a great first stop.

[http://www.edealinfo.com](<http://www.edealinfo.com/>) \- Centralized site for hot, short-term deals. They have a list of 'todays deals' and frequently email people when new deals become available. When reviewed, I saw approximately 20 vendors represented with various deals / coupons.  
**Comments:** Good deals, but I was most impressed by the Freebies. When I looked, they had Freebies listed for Pinnacle 9.0 software, a USB Bluetooth Adapter, and a wireless PS2 Controller!

[http://www.fishingfordeals.com](<http://www.fishingfordeals.com/>) \- This is the web at its finest. Nothing but a simple forum for people to share & discuss deals and coupons. If you have time to surf or don't mind registering, you can assure yourself of a steady stream of deal/coupon information!  
**Comments:** I love it... Its simple, easy to use, and actually makes me feel that I'm getting honest deals - not deals that have some sort of catch at the end.

[http://www.slickdeals.net](<http://www.slickdeals.net/>) \- Deal & Coupon listings... SlickDeals has an easy to navigate layout and immediately greets you with deals upon visiting their site. I also like the RSS feed that makes it much easier to keep up with the latest deals without clogging up your Inbox!  
**Comments:** Good site... definitely worth checking out while you're shopping. The RSS feed is a great touch. The only downside is that it had less deals/coupons than others - but they all appear to be high quality :)

[http://www.dealsea.com](<http://www.dealsea.com/>) \- Deals and Coupons! Simple layout and pretty easy to navigate. Very sparse on the actual Deals, but the Coupon section is pretty comprehensive!  
**Comments:** Similar to others... nothing special, but worth stopping by in your hunt.

[http://www.dailyedeals.com](<http://www.dailyedeals.com/>) \- Similar to the other sites... but also has a neat section for 'First Time' buyers. These are deals exclusively for new customers and they had some fresh vendor faces in there (BlueFly, CarParts.com, Chadwicks, Kenneth Cole, etc.).  
Comments: Professional layout, good categorization, and a strong list of Coupons. The actual Deals were pretty sparse and looked suspiciously like more coupons, but no matter. They cover some new vendors which is refreshing!

[http://www.keepcash.com](<http://www.keepcash.com/>) \- Deals, Deals, Deals! This site was one of the best around for Black Friday. In fact, its still showing links to existing Black Friday deals!!! The layout is great and its very professional.  
**Comments:** Looks great and the deals are good. Good balance with Coupons and Deals!

[http://www.a2zdeals.com](<http://www.a2zdeals.com/>) \- Similar to eDeals, it is a central location for Deals and Coupons.  
**Comments:** I wasn't as happy with this site as I was with others. I felt like this site was geared more towards subtle advertising rather than actual Deals. For example, it has "Walmart, Cameras starting at $48!". It also had a confusing layout... Come here after you visit others.

[http://www.goodbazaar.com](<http://www.goodbazaar.com/>) \- At first glance, it appears that GoodBazaar has a lot of deals/coupons. However, I quickly realized that most of them were simply product advertisements :(  
**Comments:** Come here as a last resort in your deal hunting excursion... There are good deals sprinkled in, but you'll have trouble easily finding them through the actual advertisements.

[http://www.pricegrabber.com](<http://www.pricegrabber.com/>) \- If you're price hunting, its difficult to beat this site. It even helps you compare your final costs by estimating shipping from each vendor!
