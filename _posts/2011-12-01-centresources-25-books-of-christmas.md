Earlier this year, we were honored to be nominated by .net magazine as Agency of the Year. We're still blown away by it, and it got us thinking:

A little while ago, .net announced their [25 favorite books](<http://www.netmagazine.com/features/top-25-books-web-designers-and-developers>) for web designers and developers. We thought their list was pretty stellar, so we decided to celebrate the holidays by giving away a copy of those books each day from December 1st to December 25th. Here's how you can win:

1) Follow us on Twitter and Like us on Facebook to be eligible to enter. It won't hurt if you add us on Google+, either!  
2) Retweet our daily message to be entered to win. Your post must include our hashtag #santasource to be eligible to enter.  
3) We'll choose a winner at random and message you if you've won the book of the day - good luck!

It's our little way of saying thanks for helping support Nashville's interactive community and for being just generally awesome people. Stay tuned to @centresource on [Twitter](<http://www.twitter.com/centresource>) or our [Facebook page](<http://www.facebook.com/centresource>) (and of course, we've already discussed that it wouldn't hurt to check our [Google+ page](<https://plus.google.com/112891722947400769727/posts>) either) for your chance to win, and have a happy holidays!
