The infamous line, "Wont you be my neighbor?" certainly came to mind when we were introduced to [NeighborMD](<http://www.neighbormd.com/>), a start up company run by former CEO of O'Charley's. With the unique vision of taking a walk-in urgent care facility and igniting it with a comfortable, "Starbucks-esque" environment, it was clear that creating an innovative web-presence was a must for NeighborMD as they looked to expand rapidly throughout middle Tennessee. 

NeighborMD relied on us to create an online marketing platform that would speak directly to their demographic and be expandable as they add new urgent care facilities. And through robust development and design efforts, NeighborMD was launched and shortly thereafter received an award from the Nashville American Marketing Association.

All in all a downright neighborly interaction.
