Kaspersky labs [reports](<https://web.archive.org/web/20060312071844/http://www.viruslist.com/en/weblog?weblogid=166186227>) on an increase in sightings of the GpCode virus, which is the [virus that encrypts files](<https://csblog.quietlife.net/2005/05/24/extortion-scheme/>) and demands a ransom to decrypt.
