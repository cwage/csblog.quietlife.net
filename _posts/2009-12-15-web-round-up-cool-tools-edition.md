_From time to time, we like to share what we have found interesting on the Web. Take a look at these and let us know what you think -- Good? Bad? Useful? Found something better? Let us know in the comments section!_

**fontcapture**  
<https://web.archive.org/web/20091215/http://www.fontcapture.com/>  
Create a font from your own handwriting. No software required. All you need is a printer and a scanner - and it's free!

**Gliffy**  
[http://www.gliffy.com](<http://www.gliffy.com/>)  
With Gliffy online diagram software, you can easily create professional-quality flowcharts, diagrams, floor plans, technical drawings, wireframes, and more.

**HTML to PDF**  
<https://web.archive.org/web/20091215/http://www.htm2pdf.co.uk/>  
Free online tool that allows you to convert HTML to PDF. Also includes a free "save as PDF" option for websites.

**Thinkature**  
<https://web.archive.org/web/20091215/http://thinkature.com/>  
Real-time collaboration/virtual team workspace for the web.

**MyStickies**  
<https://web.archive.org/web/20091215/http://www.mystickies.com/>  
Sticky notes for the web. They let you save the web for later - like bookmarks on steroids.

**feedly**  
<https://web.archive.org/web/20091215/http://www.feedly.com/>  
feedly organizes your favorite sites into a fun, magazine-like start page.

**Adobe Browserlab**  
[https://browserlab.adobe.com](<https://web.archive.org/web/20130423210304/https://browserlab.adobe.com/>)  
An easier, faster solution for cross-browser testing.  
Preview and test your web pages on leading browsers and operating systems - on demand.

**Fliggo**  
<https://web.archive.org/web/20091215/http://www.fliggo.com/>  
Build a video blog, the next YouTube, or just a private place to share videos ... in seconds.
