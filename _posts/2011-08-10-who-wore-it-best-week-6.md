Six weeks ago, Chip Hayner and I wore the exact same outfit to the office. We thought it would be fun to have a "Who Wore It Best" contest and let the people of the interwebs vote using [Survey Monkey](<http://surveymonkey.com/>). Since then, we've noticed that at least once a week, two of the guys here will show up to work wearing the exact same thing. 

Here's a look in the "Centresource Who Wore It Best" archives and their results.
