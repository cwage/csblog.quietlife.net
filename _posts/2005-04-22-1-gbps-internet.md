Want 1 Gbps symmetric bandwidth at home? [Move to Hong Kong](<https://web.archive.org/web/20050427213227/http://www.convergedigest.com/Bandwidth/newnetworksarticle.asp?ID=14545>):

> Hong Kong Broadband Network (HKBN) officially launched its 1 Gbps symmetric service for the residential market. Approximately 800,000 households, out of a total of 2.2 million households in Hong Kong, are wired to receive the service. The 1 Gbps symmetric service is priced at US$215 per month. HKBN Premium bb1000 service is being offered on the same metro Ethernet infrastructure that delivers the company's symmetric 100 Mbps (US$34/month) and symmetric 10 Mbps (US$16/month) services.
