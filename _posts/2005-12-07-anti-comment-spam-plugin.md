The ol' CentreSource Blog was the target of an increased level of [blacklist-poisoning/Joe-Job comment spam](<https://web.archive.org/web/20051126115915/http://blog.centresource.com/2005/11/14/comment-spam-joejob-example/>) all week (on the order of 20-30 a day), and I got a little fed up.

So, I wrote a Wordpress plugin with a simple yet effective goal against automated comment-spam: the requiring of a "code word" in the comment form posting.

You can get the plugin, and a more detailed explanation of how it works [here](<https://web.archive.org/web/20051230042631/http://chris.quietlife.net/2005/12/07/wordverify/>).
